For best shooting accuracy, do NOT compress your browser window. Accuracy goes down the farther you click from the character. If accuracy appears to be blatantly wrong, press and hold option+Command+i, allow the console window to appear, then close the console window. Strangely, this seems to fix the problem.
Use WASD keys to move, click to fire.
