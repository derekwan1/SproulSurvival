For best shooting accuracy, do NOT compress your browser window. If accuracy appears to be blatantly wrong, open the developer window (option+Command+c in Safari after enabling developer menu , option+Command+i in Chrome) and then close the developer window. Strangely, this seems to fix the problem.
Use WASD keys to move, click to fire.
